Common Questions About the Colombian Retirement Visa

How long does the visa process typically take?

Once all documents are correctly gathered and submitted, the Colombian government usually takes 2 to 5 weeks to process the visa. Please note that document gathering (apostilles, translations, etc.) can add several weeks to this overall timeline.

My pension income isn't from a standard source (e.g., a trust). Can I still qualify?

Often, yes. The Colombian Pensioner Visa can accommodate various regular income sources, provided they meet the minimum amount and can be officially certified and traced. We have successfully assisted clients with pensions from trusts, private investment portfolios, and other non-traditional retirement funds. We'll carefully assess your specific financial situation and documentation to build the strongest possible case.

Is health insurance mandatory for the Pensioner Visa?

Yes, a key requirement is to have a health insurance plan that provides coverage in Colombia for at least one year. We can guide you on suitable options if needed.
